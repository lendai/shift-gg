We are an organization devoted to the development and sustainability of esport. We work closely with companies to create the right foundations for esport to grow. And for them to grow with esport.

Our approach

Esports has grown exponentially the last X years. A lot of it is great. A lot of it is not. There are lots of opportunities to do good within the gaming community. We believe that small nudges in the right direction will lead to big changes. That’s why we want to create effective and smart projects/activities together with brands and others affiliated with esports that care for the community.

Initially, we will mainly focus on these three areas:

  1. An Inclusive Esport — E is for Everyone

    Our main and most important mission is to make esport a sport that is welcoming and one that unifies. All ages, nationalities, genders and personalities. Esports has the strength of being accessible to most, so let's make it enjoyable for all.

  2. A Sustainable Esport — Not all fun and games

    Esport would be nothing without its athletes, and to be able to perform in the long run it is key that physical and mental health is a core pillar of esport. For the stars of today and the talents of tomorrow.

  3. A Progressive Esport — New opportunities demands new ideas

    With a sport growing as quick as esport, it’s vital that we’re not afraid to rethink the industry standards over and over again. Nothing is to be considered finished. The only thing we now for sure is that the sport is ever shifting.

    4. Collaborate with us

    Regardless if you are someone who spends their day within the marketing department of a company, someone working in an esports organization, a streamer, a professional esports athlete or just someone who (like us) believe in growing esports, please do get in touch. We are always looking for new collaborations that further develops esports.
